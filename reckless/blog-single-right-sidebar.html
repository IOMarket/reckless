How to make a page

by ImanGM / in photography, business / 4 Comments

Az harf haee ke nemigofti, Az faslaye sard bizaaram. Man zood in panjararo bastam. To dir gofti, dooset daram. Too akharin baroone paeezi, raft oon kasi ke asheghe man bood. Man hay hay in eshgho baridam, paeez fasle gerye kardan bood. Man hay hay in eshgho baridam...

To hasti nemitarsam az bi kasi, nemitarsam az bazie sarnevesht. Nemibinamet ama hes mikonam, kenaram ghadam mizani ta behesht. Be man yad midi sabouri konam, nemizari az zendegi khaste sham, ba inke havaye jahan khoob nist, be eshghe to daram nafas mikesham...

Az vaghti too paeez, del kandi, hey too otagham barg mirizam. Yek fasl poshte panjeram boodi, yek omre too in khoone paeeze. Taghsire man boode ageh sardi, taghsire man boode ageh khastam. Paeez too otaghe man habse, man zood in panjararo bastam...
